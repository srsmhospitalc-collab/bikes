2026 Me Lene Wali Top 5 Bikes: Mileage, Price Aur Features

Agar aap 2026 me nayi bike lene ka plan bana rahe hain to ye article aapke liye hai. Aaj hum baat karenge un 5 bikes ke baare me jo mileage, looks, aur price teeno me best hain. Chahe aap college student ho ya office jana ho, inme se koi na koi bike aapke budget me fit ho jayegi.

1. Hero Splendor Plus XTEC - Mileage Ka Baap

Ex-Showroom Price: ₹78,000 se shuru
Mileage: 70-75 kmpl ka dawa
Engine: 97.2cc, Air Cooled
Kyu Kharide: Agar aapka daily 40-50 km chalna hai aur petrol ka kharcha bachana hai to Splendor se best kuch nahi. Naye XTEC model me Bluetooth connectivity, digital meter aur LED headlight bhi mil jata hai.

2026 ke update me iska engine aur smooth ho gaya hai. Gaon ho ya sheher, har jagah iske part aasani se mil jate hain. Middle class family ki pehli pasand ab bhi yahi hai.

2. Bajaj Pulsar N160 - Style Aur Power Ka Combo

Ex-Showroom Price: ₹1.30 Lakh approx
Mileage: 45-50 kmpl
Engine: 164.82cc, Oil Cooled
Kyu Kharide: College ke ladko ki dream bike. 160cc ka power, dual-channel ABS, USD forks aur killer looks. Raat me LED DRL dekh ke log piche mud ke dekhenge.

Pulsar N160 ka pickup aur stability dono zabardast hai. Highway pe 90-100 ki speed pe bhi bike hilti nahi. Haan mileage Splendor jaisi mat expect karna, ye power ke liye bani hai.

3. TVS Raider 125 - Features Loaded Machine

Ex-Showroom Price: ₹95,000 se shuru
Mileage: 55-60 kmpl
Engine: 124.8cc, Air Cooled
Kyu Kharide: 1 Lakh ke budget me sabse zyada feature. 5-inch TFT screen, voice assist, ride modes, aur 125cc segment ka best engine. Looks me ye 150cc wali bike ko takkar deti hai.

4. Yamaha MT-15 V2 - Street King

Ex-Showroom Price: ₹1.68 Lakh
Mileage: 40-45 kmpl
Engine: 155cc, Liquid Cooled, VVA
Kyu Kharide: Agar budget hai aur hatke aggressive look chahiye to MT-15 lo. Liquid cooled engine, USD fork, dual-channel ABS aur Yamaha ki reliability. Sheher ke traffic me chalaney ka maza alag hi hai.

5. Royal Enfield Hunter 350 - Bullet Wala Swag Kam Daam Me

Ex-Showroom Price: ₹1.50 Lakh se shuru
Mileage: 35-37 kmpl
Engine: 349cc, Air-Oil Cooled
Kyu Kharide: "Bullet leni hai par Classic 350 mehngi lagti hai" walo ke liye perfect. Hunter halki hai, sheher me chalani easy hai, aur dug-dug ki awaaz bhi aati hai. 2026 me iske naye Rebel colours bhi aaye hain.

Final Baat: Bike Lene Se Pehle Ye Check Kar Lena

1. EMI vs Cash: EMI pe 20-30k extra chala jata hai. Ho sake to cash lo.
2. Service Center: Ghar ke 5-7 km me company ka service center hai ya nahi check kar lo.
3. Test Ride: YouTube pe review dekh ke bike mat lena. 2-3 km chala ke dekho, seat comfortable hai ya nahi.
4. Insurance + RTO: On-road price me ye dono add hote hain. Dealer se full breakup maangna.

Kagaz pe 70 ka mileage aur real life me 55 ka mileage - ye normal hai bhai. Traffic aur chalaney ka tarika sabse zyada matter karta hai.
